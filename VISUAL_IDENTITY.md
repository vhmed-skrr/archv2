## üì∏ Photography Standards

### Lighting Requirements
- **Golden Hour ONLY**: Sunrise or sunset lighting
- **Warm Color Temperature**: 3000K-4500K
- **Directional Light**: Strong shadows for depth and drama
- **God Rays**: Atmospheric light beams when appropriate
- **No Harsh Midday Sun**: Avoid flat, overexposed lighting

### Color Palette (Strict)
**Allowed Colors:**
- **Gold**: `#D4AF37`, `#FFD700`, `#C5A572`
- **Orange**: `#E67E22`, `#D35400`, `#F39C12`
- **Sandy Beige**: `#E8D5B7`, `#C9B18F`, `#A89968`
- **Warm Browns**: `#8B7355`, `#6B5D4F`
- **Deep Shadows**: `#2C1810`, `#1A0F08`

**Forbidden:**
- Cool blues (except sky gradients)
- Greens (except minimal palm trees)
- Bright whites
- Neon or artificial colors

## üö´ Strict Exclusions

**NEVER Include:**
- People (tourists, visitors, archaeologists)
- Modern buildings or structures
- Vehicles (cars, buses, camels with riders)
- Contemporary objects (phones, signs, fences)
- Text overlays or captions
- Watermarks
- Crowds or groups
- Scaffolding or restoration equipment

## üé¨ Composition Guidelines

### Framing
- **Rule of Thirds**: Classical composition
- **Leading Lines**: Use architectural elements
- **Symmetry**: Center monuments for impact
- **Negative Space**: Allow monuments to breathe
- **Low Angles**: Emphasize monumentality

### Depth & Atmosphere
- **Foreground Interest**: Sand dunes, stone fragments
- **Atmospheric Haze**: Subtle desert atmosphere
- **Deep Shadows**: Create mystery and drama
- **Texture Detail**: Sharp focus on stone surfaces

## üñºÔ∏è Usage by Section

### 1. Hero Sections
- **Subject**: Pyramids at sunset, temple facades
- **Mood**: Epic, awe-inspiring
- **Composition**: Wide panoramic shots
- **Example**: Pyramids silhouetted against orange sky

### 2. Era Cards
- **Ancient Egypt**: Temple columns with hieroglyphs
- **Greco-Roman**: Alexandria ruins with golden light
- **Coptic**: Ancient church interior, warm candlelight
- **Islamic**: Mosque domes and minarets at dusk
- **Modern**: Museum artifacts with dramatic lighting

### 3. Background Images
- **Subtle**: Low opacity (20-40%)
- **Texture**: Stone, papyrus, sand
- **Non-distracting**: Support content, don't compete

### 4. Icons & Symbols
- **Style**: Simplified, elegant line art
- **Color**: Single color (gold or navy)
- **Background**: Transparent PNG
- **Examples**: Eye of Horus, Ankh, Scarab
