Project History

The journey of digitalizing Egypt's timeless legacy

2024: Inception

The Egyptian Heritage project was conceived by a group of archaeologists and software engineers with a shared vision: to make Egypt's history accessible to everyone, everywhere.

2025: Development

Development of the core platform began, focusing on creating a robust, multilingual architecture capable of hosting high-fidelity images and detailed 3D models of artifacts.

2026: Launch

The platform officially launched, featuring a curated selection of Egypt's most significant historical sites, from the Giza Plateau to the Temples of Luxor.
